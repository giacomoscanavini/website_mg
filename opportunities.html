Postgraduate scholarships

Prospective Ph.D. students

I encourage students to contact me and discuss their research proposals before applying.

The standard Ph.D. scholarship at HKU is approximately $19,000 HKD per month. HKU-PS and HKPFS scholarships provide higher salary and travel grants.

Note that the application deadline of the main round is December 1st.

You may still contact me to apply in the second round if you miss the main round deadline but meet all admission requirements of the university: HKU admission requirements.

Postdoc fellowships

Hong Kong RGC Junior Research Fellow Scheme

Interested postdoctoral candidates are welcome to contact me about the Hong Kong RGC Junior Research Fellow Scheme.

The nomination deadline is usually in late October; do not hesitate to contact me if you are interested.
